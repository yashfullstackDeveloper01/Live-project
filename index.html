About Renuka Dairy

At Renuka Dairy, we are committed to delivering the freshest and highest quality milk products sourced directly from local farmers. Our mission is to provide nutritious, wholesome dairy to every home, ensuring health and happiness for our community. With over 20 years of experience in the dairy industry, we have built strong relationships with farmers across the region, ensuring that our milk comes from healthy, well-cared-for cows raised on sustainable farms.
